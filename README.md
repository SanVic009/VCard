# VCard: Smart Business Card Scanner & Company Profiler

VCard is a mobile application designed to bridge the gap between physical networking and deep business intelligence. It does not just save contact information—it automatically researches and profiles the companies behind your contacts, providing you with real-time insights right in your pocket.

---

## Key Features

### 1. Smart Card Capture
* **Instant Digitization:** Quickly snap a photo of any physical business card using the device's camera.
* **Flexible Input:** Import card images directly from your device gallery if you've already saved them.

### 2. AI-Driven Contact Extraction
* **Zero Manual Typing:** Sophisticated vision-language processing automatically analyzes scanned card images.
* **Structured Data:** Instantly extracts key information:
  * Full Name & Job Title
  * Company Name
  * Multiple Email Addresses & Phone Numbers
  * Company Websites & Physical Address
* **Interactive Correction:** A verification screen lets you review, modify, append, or delete extracted fields before saving to ensure 100% accuracy.

### 3. Web-Grounded Company Enrichment
* **Real-time Internet Research:** As soon as a card is saved, VCard performs search-grounded research on the company name or website.
* **Detailed Corporate Profiles:** Automatically discovers and displays:
  * **Product Catalog:** Names and descriptions of key products.
  * **Service Directory:** Descriptions of services offered.
  * **Technology Stack:** Software, programming languages, frameworks, cloud providers, and tools used by the company.
* **Status Tracking:** Keep track of the research progress via live status updates (Pending, Completed, Failed).
